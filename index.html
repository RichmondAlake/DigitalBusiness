    Augmented Reality and the Internet of Things

    IOT is the main focus of industrial manufacturing industry, alongside big data. Gaining informational data from machinery that are connected is very useful within industrial sectors, as it helps with maintenance of equipment and improvement of services delivered. But with a lot of data collected that are limited to the space of our Monitors and device displays, visual representation of big data can become a complication. Augmented reality solves this problem by utilising the space that is essentially our reality, in fact limitless space. We can display information based on location and time, ensuring the user will always get the relevant information.
